=======

components/auth/AuthHeader.tsx: For the "Create a new account" or "Sign in to your account" headings and the "Already have an account?" / "Don't have an account?" links.

components/auth/AuthLayout.tsx: This will contain the main structural layout (the gradient background, logo, and the two-panel grid), which is reusable for both login and registration.

components/auth/AuthResetPassword.tsx: Contains the "Reset Password" form.

components/auth/DescriptivePanel.tsx: The right-hand panel with the illustration and marketing text.

components/auth/FormInput.tsx: A reusable component for a form field (label, input, and message), making the form rendering cleaner.

components/auth/LegalDisclaimer.tsx: The small text at the bottom about Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

components/auth/LoginEmailForm.tsx: Contains the full email login form (email, password). This will receive onSubmit and form state props.

components/auth/LoginMainOptions.tsx: Encapsulates the initial view with social login buttons and the "Continue with email" option.

components/auth/PasswordRequirements.tsx: A dedicated component to display the password strength requirements.

components/auth/RegistrationEmailForm.tsx: Contains the full email registration form (name, email, password fields, password requirements, and submit button). This will receive onSubmit and form state props.

components/auth/RegistrationMainOptions.tsx: Encapsulates the initial view with social login buttons and the "Continue with email" option.

components/auth/SocialButtons.tsx: This will encapsulate the Google, LinkedIn, Facebook, and "Continue with Email" buttons, along with their respective icons. This block is highly reusable.
